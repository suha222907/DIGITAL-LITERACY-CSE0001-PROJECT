SOCIAL MEDIA DO'S AND DONT'S CHECKLIST 
(FOR COLLEGE STUDENTS)

DO’s:

1. Think before posting
2. Use strong passwords
3. Respect others online
4. Check privacy settings
5. Use social media for learning

DON’Ts:

1. Don’t share personal details
2. Don’t spread fake news
3. Don’t post offensive content
4. Don’t accept unknown requests
5. Don’t overuse social media
